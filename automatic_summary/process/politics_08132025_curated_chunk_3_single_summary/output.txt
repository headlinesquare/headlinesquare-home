## 1. The Presidency & Executive Branch

### 1.1 Federal Control & Policing of Washington D.C.
The federal takeover of the Washington D.C. police force continues to expand, with President Trump signaling his intent to extend the operation beyond its initial 30-day mandate and expressing his expectation that Congress will grant the authorization [1]. The stated goal is to maintain federal patrols "all day, all night for at least a month" [2]. Early results from the operation include 66 arrests, with an influx of more National Guard troops expected to bolster the federal presence [3]. The unprecedented nature of this action is prompting legal analysis regarding whether a similar federal takeover of local law enforcement could be replicated in other American cities [4]. This move has drawn sharp criticism, with some reports alleging it was encouraged by corporate lobbyists, while other academic experts frame it as part of a national "slide towards fascism" [5, 6].
