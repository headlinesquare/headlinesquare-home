The judge, who one report says "blistered" the administration, warned that if the President’s approach were allowed to stand, "'everyone would be at risk'" [2]. Beyond immigration, legal battles over social issues are also proceeding. South Carolina is escalating a case to the U.S. Supreme Court, asking it to intervene to prevent a transgender teenager from using a school bathroom [3].

Secretary Noem has further stated that the administration has not taken any options "off the table" concerning the expansion of these operations to other American cities [2].
