The swift breakdown from a declared diplomatic achievement to open conflict signifies a severe escalation in the region.

## 4. Congressional and Political Affairs

### 4.1 GOP "Megabill" Stalls on Procedural Hurdles

The Republican majority’s primary legislative vehicle, a "megabill" intended to pass via budget reconciliation, is facing critical procedural roadblocks from the Senate Parliamentarian. The parliamentarian has ruled against key conservative priorities, finding that a proposed "civil service overhaul" and provisions to expand offshore oil and gas drilling violate the Byrd Rule and therefore cannot be included in the bill [1, 2]. A separate ruling also blocked an effort to force the U.S. Postal Service to scrap its electric vehicle fleet [3]. These procedural defeats weaken the package and exacerbate political tensions within the GOP. One Republican senator has already warned that proposed Medicaid cuts could "derail" the entire legislative effort, highlighting the fragile nature of the majority coalition [4].
