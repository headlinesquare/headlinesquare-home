A Minnesota man has spoken out about how his student visa revocation "shattered" his life [2]. The deportation process has been marked by errors, with thousands of people, including U.S. citizens, reportedly receiving surprise deportation notices by email [3]. Meanwhile, the government has spent over $500,000 on hyper-targeted YouTube advertisements designed to discourage irregular migration [6].
