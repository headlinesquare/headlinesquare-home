The Trump administration has enacted a significant and rapid shift in federal oversight of Washington D.C., triggering a direct legal and political confrontation with the city's government. The D.C. government has filed a lawsuit against the administration, legally challenging what its filing terms an "'unlawful' federal takeover" of its local policing authority [3]. This legal battle is already attracting sharp political commentary, with at least one headline framing the lawsuit, led by D.C. Attorney General Brian Schwalb, as a deliberate effort to preserve what it calls a "crime-infested craphole" [4].
