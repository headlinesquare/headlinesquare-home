## 2. Economic Policies and Impacts

### 2.1 Tariffs and Labor Market
Trump's tariffs are creating significant economic disruption. U.S. ports are experiencing slowdowns, described as "a warning of rough economic seas ahead" [1], while factories needing Chinese machinery are in a holding pattern [2]. CNN suggests these tariffs could ironically benefit Europe more than America regarding inflation control [3]. Despite these challenges, April's jobs report showed stronger-than-expected growth with 177,000 jobs added [5], though some sources characterize hiring as having "slowed" amid tariff turmoil [6]. Trump called on the Federal Reserve to lower interest rates following the report [7].
