## 1. The Trump Administration's Legislative Agenda

### 1.1 The Omnibus "Megabill": Economic and Fiscal Policy

Senate Republicans have officially released a massive, 940-page omnibus bill designed to enact key components of President Trump's legislative agenda [1]. The legislation, which the President has publicly dubbed his "big, beautiful bill," contains a sweeping overhaul of the federal budget, combining significant tax cuts with new spending policies, and is being fast-tracked for a vote this weekend [2]. A central controversy surrounding the bill involves its fiscal accounting. Reports allege that trillions of dollars in future debt have been strategically excluded from official cost calculations, a move critics claim is intended to make the bill appear cheaper than it is [3]. Further analysis of the bill's contents suggests the inclusion of "strongarm tax tactics" that could disproportionately benefit tech billionaires [4].

Senate Republicans are preparing for a weekend work session to meet a firm deadline imposed by President Trump for passing his signature tax-and-spending bill [1].
