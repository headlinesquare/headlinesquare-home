## 1. International Relations and Foreign Policy

### 1.1 Ukraine-Russia Conflict

President Trump's approach to the Ukraine-Russia conflict has triggered significant international concern. U.S. allies have expressed alarm at his plan to let Russia keep most Ukrainian-seized territory [1], while Trump has explicitly blamed Ukraine for initiating the war and declared "Crimea will stay with Russia" [2]. Despite campaign promises, Trump has privately acknowledged ending the Ukraine war has proven more difficult than anticipated [3]. He has characterized Russia not completely taking over Ukraine as a "concession" [4] and admitted his pledge to end the war on his first day in office was "said in jest" [5].
