## 2. Immigration and Citizenship Policies

### 2.1 Birthright Citizenship Supreme Court Case

The Supreme Court has scheduled May oral arguments on President Trump's plan to restrict birthright citizenship [1]. While allowing the challenge to proceed, the Court has maintained a hold on implementing the restrictions until a decision is rendered [2]. This case represents a fundamental constitutional question about the 14th Amendment and whether the administration can limit automatic citizenship for children born on U.S. soil to non-citizen parents [3]. The May timeline suggests a ruling could come by the end of the Court's term in June 2025, setting up a momentous constitutional showdown [4].
