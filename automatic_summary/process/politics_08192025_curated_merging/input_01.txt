## 1. Foreign Policy and International Relations

### 1.1 Ukraine-Russia Diplomatic Efforts and U.S. Involvement

The White House is spearheading a major diplomatic initiative to broker peace between Russia and Ukraine. The effort began with a direct call from President Trump to President Putin to arrange a face-to-face meeting with Ukrainian President Zelenskyy [1]. Subsequent talks held in Washington, D.C. included a pledge of long-term U.S. support for Ukraine, a move intended to bolster Kyiv's position [2]. However, analyses of the summit's results are mixed. While the high-level engagement provided a symbolic lift for Ukraine, some assessments describe the concrete outcomes as "thin," signaling a difficult diplomatic road ahead [3]. The situation's complexity is heightened by a report that President Trump was caught on a hot microphone stating his belief that President Putin "wants to make a deal for me," a comment that adds a layer of personal political dynamic to the international negotiations [4].
