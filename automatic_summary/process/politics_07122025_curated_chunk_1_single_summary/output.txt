## 1. Immigration and Border Policy

### 1.1 ICE Enforcement, Raids, and Incidents

Federal immigration enforcement in Southern California has escalated into a series of critical incidents. Recent large-scale ICE raids on two area farms resulted in the arrests of approximately 200 immigrants [1]. The operations turned deadly at a Ventura County cannabis farm, where one farmworker was killed after a fall from a greenhouse during the raid, and others were reported unaccounted for, according to a union source [2, 3]. The most contentious incident involved the detention of a disabled U.S. citizen veteran during a raid in Camarillo, an event that sparked immediate family outcry [4]. Separately, a headline attributes a claim to Kristi Noem that ICE officers have been doxxed by "antifa, anarchists in Portland" [5].
