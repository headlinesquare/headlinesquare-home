## 3. Economic Policy and Trade War

### 3.1 Tariff Implementation

A significant confrontation has erupted between the White House and Amazon over tariff pricing transparency. Amazon considered showing customers how much of a product's price comes from recently implemented tariffs [1], but abandoned the plan after the White House labeled it "hostile" [2]. President Trump reportedly personally complained to Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos about the idea [3]. The administration's tariffs reach as high as 145% on Chinese imports, affecting approximately 70% of Amazon's product offerings [4].
