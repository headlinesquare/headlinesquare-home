The White House is actively considering a full spectrum of military options against Iran. ## 3. Congressional Affairs

### 3.1 Republican Budget Bill Faces Headwinds

The Republican majority’s large-scale budget reconciliation bill—which one headline cites President Trump as calling the "One Big, Beautiful Bill"—is encountering serious procedural and political obstacles in the Senate. The Senate Parliamentarian has issued crucial advice that several of the bill's key provisions are not permissible under the Byrd Rule, and therefore cannot be passed via the protected reconciliation process [1]. Separately, the bill faces internal GOP opposition, with "Senate conservatives [who] demand changes" before they will offer their support [2]. The legislation's wide-ranging impacts reportedly affect the Affordable Care Act and could "derail hundreds of planned clean energy projects," described by critics as "‘Throwing us off a cliff’" [3, 4].
