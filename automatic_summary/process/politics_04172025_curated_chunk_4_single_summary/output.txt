## 2. Immigration Enforcement and Border Policy

### 2.1 ICE Detentions of American Citizens

Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) has generated controversy through multiple detentions of American citizens. Juan Carlos Gomez-Lopez, a U.S.-born American citizen, remains in ICE custody despite a county judge finding his birth certificate "authentic" [2]. Mahmoud Khalil published a Washington Post opinion piece questioning what his detention says about America [4]. These cases highlight growing tensions between aggressive immigration enforcement and civil liberties protections.
